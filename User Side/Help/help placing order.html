Placing an order

It's easy to place an order with us! Follow these 3 simple steps.


Step 1. Browse and shop our vast range of items.

You can navigate the website using the navigation bar at the top of each page. This includes access to search, help, your
shopping bag, your account information and all of your favorite shopping departments within Next.

You can search our site by item description, item number or keyword. To view a product, simply click on any picture to see an
enlarged view and other details including a description, sizes and colours. Then simply select a size, at which point we will let
you know if the item is in stock. You can then and "Add to Bag" to purchase the item.



Step 2. Checkout

Once you have added all of your items to your bag, you simply need to click 'Checkout' and follow the steps.

Step 3. Decide how you want to pay.

You can pay in full by Visa/ master card.
