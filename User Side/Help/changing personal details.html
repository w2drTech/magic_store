Changing personal details

It’s essential to keep your personal details up to date so we can keep in touch with you.
Sign into ‘My Account’ online and select ‘Change Details’ from the menu on the left-hand side.






If you’re unable to update your details via ‘My Account’ please contact one of the team on 0333 777 8788*.
To change your name you will need to contact us by email, phone or chat. You will need to confirm the reason for the name change.
If it’s a change of name by Deed Poll, we will require a copy of the Deed Poll certificate to be sent to us.
