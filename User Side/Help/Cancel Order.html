I want to cancel my order. How can I do this?

We know that sometimes you can change your mind. So, your order can be cancelled within one hour of the order being placed.
To cancel your order within the hour:

. Sign into'My Account' and click on'Order History.'

. You'll see a list of your Orders, then click and press on the "Cancel Order".

. Or you can cancel one item by press on the close mark in front of a item
