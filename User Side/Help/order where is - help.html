Where’s my order ?

Any orders placed will appear on your account. You can also track any outstanding orders here.
To track your order:

1.Log in to My Account using your email address or customer number and password

2.Select ‘My Orders’ from the menu on the left-hand side

Here you can view:

• Up-to-date parcel statuses

• The delivery destination


(This information is updated at regular intervals)

How many days to deliver to my Address ?

Orders will be delivered to the location provided by you within 6-8 working days

what will be my delivery cost ?

Delivery Cost
1.If the delivery location is within Colombo - delivery cost of Rs.300/- added to the total order value.
2.-If the delivery location is out of Colombo - delivery cost of Rs.450/- added to the total order value.
