How do I return?

I want to return my items. How can I do this?

We accept returns via our Stores and SL Post.


Please return items in a new and unused condition and wherever possible in the original packaging. Items must be returned within
28 days of being received. If you return items after 28 days we will issue you with an eVoucher. The 28 days start from the day
after the items were delivered to your home address, or if delivered to the store, the 28 days start from the day after the items were collected.


We’re working towards reducing the amount of plastic we use. When returning your items please put all items into one parcel bag and
attach one returns label (regardless of the items coming back.) We’ll do the rest - once your parcel reaches us we’ll scan each
item individually and credit them.

To return any items you no longer wish to keep, just follow one of the steps below.
