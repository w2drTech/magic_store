Placing an order

After placing an order, you'll receive an 'Order Confirmation' email within 1 hour. This will include details of all the items you have ordered
with expected delivery dates.

If you've not received the order confirmation after 1 hour, check your junk folder to ensure it hasn't filtered into there.

it's also worth checking the email address you've provided, just incase it's not quite right .for more information send email click here.

if you have not received an order confirmation email then you can also check your order was successful by signing into'My Account'
(user account) and selecting 'MyOrders.'The details will appear here within two hours of the order being placed.

Still not able to see details of your order? Don't worry, get in touch and a member of the team will be happy to check on your order for you.
